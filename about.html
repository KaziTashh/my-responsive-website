About this guide

Built for Planning a Trip, Not Just Reading About One

Nizam’s Chronicle started as a simple frustration: most "top places to visit" pages online are either outdated, copy-pasted from each other, or missing the one thing a traveler actually needs before leaving the hotel — current opening hours and entry fees.

This guide covers nine landmarks in detail rather than fifty in passing. Every timing and ticket price listed here was checked against official sources before publishing, and each entry links out to a map so you can see exactly where it sits relative to everything else.

How it's put together

What This Guide Tries to Get Right

Verified Details

Hours, fees, and locations are cross-checked against official tourism and monument sources rather than copied from older listicles.

Depth Over Volume

Nine landmarks, covered properly, beat a fifty-item list that repeats the same three photos and no real detail.

Practical First

Every entry answers the question a traveler actually has: when can I go, what will it cost, and how do I get there.

Free & Independent

No sponsored placements or pay-to-rank listings. This is a personal project, not a booking platform.

Kept Current

Ticket prices and timings change. When they do, the plan is to update this guide rather than let it go stale.

Built in the Open

This site is a front-end portfolio project — the code is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, no framework required.

Spot Something Out of Date?

Ticket prices and timings at Indian monuments change more often than most travel sites admit. If something here looks wrong, I'd genuinely like to know.
